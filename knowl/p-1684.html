Although the legend is interesting, you need not worry about the world ending any time soon. The number of moves required to correctly move a tower of 64 disks is \(2^{64}-1 = 18,446,744,073,709,551,615\text{.}\) At a rate of one move per second, that is \(584,942,417,355\) years! Clearly there is more to this puzzle than meets the eye.
