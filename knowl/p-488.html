For C++, a library titled C-Turtle is used to provide an equivalent of Python's Turtles. It acts as a close replacement to provide easy to use graphics to C++, while maintaining the objects and commands present in Python. It was developed by Jesse Walker-Schadler at Berea College during the summer of 2019 and can be found on GitHub here: https://github.com/walkerje/C-Turtle/ 1
