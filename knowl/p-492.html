Turtles are based on the following premise: “There is a turtle on a canvas with a colored pen attached to their tail.” In this case, the canvas is a TurtleScreen. This Turtle will follow any command you give it, which consist of telling it to go certain directions, what color of pen to use, when to raise or lower its pen, and others. Table 1.14.1 has an outline of commonly used methods when working with turtles.
