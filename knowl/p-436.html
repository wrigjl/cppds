Flowchart representing an inheritance hierarchy for logic gates. The topmost block is labeled 'Logic Gate', which branches down into two categories: 'Binary Gate' and 'Unary Gate'. Under 'Binary Gate', two further blocks represent 'AND' and 'OR' gates, each accompanied by their respective symbols. To the right, under 'Unary Gate', there's a block for the 'NOT' gate with its symbol. The structure implies that 'AND' and 'OR' gates inherit from 'Binary Gate', which along with 'Unary Gate' inherits from 'Logic Gate'.
