You can hear and see all of these videos simultaneously, but only because your computer switches from one task to another so fast that it appears seamless. Just like you, your computer gives each task some time to use its resources and cycles between them really fast. Timesharing explains why a computer slows down when there are a bunch of tabs open on a browser: the more things you have to switch between, the more time it takes to finish them.
